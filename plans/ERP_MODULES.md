# ERP System Modules and Components

This document outlines the core modules of an ERP system and their detailed components and functionality.

---

## 1. Financial Management

### 1.1 General Ledger
- Chart of accounts management
- Journal entries (manual and automated)
- Multi-currency support
- Period closing and year-end processing
- Financial statements generation (Balance Sheet, Income Statement, Cash Flow)
- Inter-company transactions
- Audit trail and transaction history

### 1.2 Accounts Payable
- Vendor master data management
- Invoice processing and matching (2-way, 3-way)
- Payment scheduling and processing
- Early payment discounts tracking
- Aging reports
- 1099 / tax document generation
- Vendor statement reconciliation

### 1.3 Accounts Receivable
- Customer master data management
- Invoice generation and delivery
- Credit management and credit limits
- Collections management
- Cash application and payment matching
- Aging analysis and dunning letters
- Bad debt management and write-offs

### 1.4 Cash Management
- Bank account management
- Bank reconciliation
- Cash flow forecasting
- Electronic fund transfers
- Positive pay file generation
- Petty cash management

### 1.5 Fixed Assets
- Asset master records
- Depreciation calculation (multiple methods)
- Asset acquisition and disposal
- Asset transfers and splits
- Revaluation processing
- Insurance and maintenance tracking

### 1.6 Budgeting and Planning
- Budget creation and approval workflows
- Budget vs actual analysis
- Rolling forecasts
- What-if scenarios
- Departmental budget allocation
- Capital expenditure planning

### 1.7 Tax Management
- Tax code configuration
- Sales tax / VAT calculation
- Tax reporting and compliance
- Withholding tax management
- Tax jurisdiction management

## Module Dependencies and Integration Points

| Module | Key Integration Points |
|--------|----------------------|
| Financial Management | All modules (as system of record for financial data) |
| HCM | Finance (payroll journal), Projects (labor cost) |
| SCM | Finance (AP/AR), Manufacturing, Sales |
| Manufacturing | SCM (inventory), Finance (costing), Quality |
| Sales | Finance (AR), SCM (inventory), CRM |
| CRM | Sales, Service, Marketing |
| Projects | Finance, HCM, Procurement |
| BI/Analytics | All modules (data source) |
